ELLA
MODELS

NEW YORK

The ELLA model, or "Efficient Lifelong Learning Algorithm," is a framework designed for continuous, multitask learning. It allows AI systems to learn new tasks while preserving knowledge from previous ones, avoiding the common problem of "catastrophic forgetting." By efficiently adapting to new data and environments, ELLA ensures that AI systems can grow and evolve over time without losing valuable information.
